    • Aim of the experiment

    • To help students visualize and comprehend the various factors that affect photosynthesis,
such as light intensity, temperature, and carbon dioxide levels.

    • To enhance students' knowledge and critical thinking skills by allowing them to experiment with 
different variables and observe the outcomes in a controlled simulation.
